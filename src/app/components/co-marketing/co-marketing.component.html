Co-Marketing with BuiltShops

Co-marketing with direct marketing can create significant new opportunities for you and BuiltShops. Co-marketing can bring many benefits:

A well-qualified introduction to one another's client. By sending co-branded direct marketing to one another's client lists, we are effectively introducing one another - and gaining the interest of each other's market, who already hold affinity and loyalty for one of you - and this will extend to both of you in important ways.

Marketing budget extension. With co-branding, direct marketing costs are shared between both parties, greatly extending the marketing budget. More budget means more creativity, more frequency and most importantly, more reach!
